My Hobby

Reading Books

I love books. Reading enables me to discover different kind of worlds which one lifetime would not have been enough I were to discover all of them in real life. I read almost about everything but I have preferences for romantic novels or spiritual books written in the English language. For me, reading is a way of life. A day without reading is a day gone waste. Reading improves my writing skills since I am exposed to a range of vocabulary and expressions which I usually use in my school essays. I love discovering new words and learning about new things. When I start reading a book, I usually forget around me and enter the book. Each word transports me into the journey that the book has to offer.
