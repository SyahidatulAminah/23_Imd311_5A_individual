My Experience

I worked on a project called extension services where we served as part of a library. An Extension Service programme with the children of Al-Nasuha Sanctuary was successfully carried out on Friday, May 19, 2023. IM 2 YOU with Al-Nasuha was the title of the programme. "IM 2 YOU with Al-Nasuha" is a programme launched exclusively for the community of Al-Nasuha Batu Cave Sanctuary in Kuala Lumpur, Selangor. The goal of this activity was to introduce and share with the community the Al-Nasuha Sanctuary's the environment and services in the Tengku Anis Library of Universiti Teknologi Mara (UiTM) Kelantan Branch Machang Campus. The manager and the children were pleased with the programme.
